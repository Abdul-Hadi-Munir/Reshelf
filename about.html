About Us

Big Savings. Zero Waste. Real Impact.

Who We Are

Reshelf is Pakistan’s first surplus-based shopping platform designed to bring the best deals directly from suppliers, distributors, and manufacturers to customers — while reducing national food waste.

Our model helps companies clear shelf-ready stock at huge discounts instead of letting it go to waste. Customers enjoy 30%–70% cheaper prices, and the environment benefits from reduced waste.

Our Mission

Reduce Waste

Fighting Pakistan’s 27% food waste crisis through sustainable supply chain recovery.

Help Consumers Save

Giving people access to perfectly good surplus goods at unbeatable prices.

Support Businesses

Helping brands clear excess inventory & recover value instead of discarding it.
