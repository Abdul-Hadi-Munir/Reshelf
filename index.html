Our Story

The journey from a simple observation to a nationwide mission.

Abdul Hadi Munir

Founder & CEO, Reshelf

"It all started with a trip to the mall. I saw a store giving massive discounts on food that was close to its expiry date. While the savings were great, I noticed there was no system to check if the products were still truly high-quality. It was a gamble for the customers."

That observation sparked an idea. I began researching and was shocked to learn that nearly 27% (approx 1.7 Billion $ worth) of food is wasted in Pakistan every year. So much of this waste includes perfectly good, packaged items from trusted brands that are simply surplus or short-dated.

This is where Reshelf was born. We built a brand to bridge this gap, but with one crucial difference: an absolute commitment to quality and trust.

Our 5-Step Verification Promise

Before any product is 'reshelved', it goes through our rigorous 5-step verification process to ensure it meets our high standards:

  • Labelling Test: We confirm all information on the packaging is clear, correct, and untampered with.
  • Taste Test: We conduct sensory evaluations to ensure the product's flavor profile is exactly as the manufacturer intended.
  • Smell Test: Our team checks for any off-odors to guarantee freshness.
  • Texture Test: We verify that the product's physical consistency meets quality standards.
  • Batch Confirmation: Most importantly, we confirm the product batch with the brand to ensure authenticity and traceability.

Reshelf is more than just a marketplace; it's a movement towards a more sustainable Pakistan, where everyone can access quality food at fair prices, and nothing good goes to waste.
